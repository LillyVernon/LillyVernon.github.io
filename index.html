Save time and money, chat with Eva now

Eva is a medical chatbot created by a group of students from the University of the West Indies for the UWI medical center. Eva is designed to automate and streamline various processes within the daily operations of the UWI Health Centre.

About Who We Are & What We Do

We are a group of university students that saw a vision for the UWI Health Center where students could create appointments and ask questions without the hassle of long waiting lines, waiting time on phone calls or having to physically go to the health centre to accomplish these tasks. We developed Eva to automate and expedite the tasks of setting appointments, requesting forms and getting answers to FAQs.
